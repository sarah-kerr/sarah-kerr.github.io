Special Expertise

While I have written on many subjects and love exploring new topics, these are some areas in which I stand out.

Prior to becoming a writer, I worked as a baker for many years, and I remain passionate about researching and trying new recipes, tools, and techiques. The many articles I have written about cooking, recipes, and similar subjects attest to my expertise in this area.

Health

At my university, I earned a B.A. in Public Health. Because of my education and training, I am able to write informative pieces on such topics as healthy diet, pregnancy, personal and public hygiene, etc.

Gardening

As an avid gardener, I am love working with the soil and enjoying its fruits. I am knowledgable about organic growing and pest control, seasonal and regional gardening, gardening tools and techniques, and many other subjects.

Weddings

One of my earliest jobs as a writer involved regular articles for a wedding planning company. I still write for this client, and over time I have accumulated considerable expertise in wedding planning and design, seasonal fashions, and similar topics.
